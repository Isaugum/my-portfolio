About me

From a young age, I was interested in literature and other arts - which led to my enrollment in Secondary School of Design and Photography in Ljubljana - while my enjoyment of natural and human sciences developed slowly and matured in college.

I am a big enjoyer of board games, interesting discussions and dissecting how and why things function the way they do. My best trait is probably being a very good team player who enjoys working on larger projects. I have good communication skills and always keep an open mind, ready to see what facts are important even when obscured by their presentation.
