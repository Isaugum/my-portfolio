About me

I was born in 1995 in Ljubljana and lived most of my life here. From a young age, I was interested in literature and other arts - which led to my enrollment in Secondary School of Design and Photography in Ljubljana - while my enjoyment of natural and human sciences developed slowly and matured in college (Faculties for Architecture and Arts).

My first experiences with coding began in early 2022, when I designed and wrote a text-based game for a friend's art project. After that, I decided to pursue more knowlege in the field of development. It seems like coding brings together the best of all worlds for me - a gold mine of interesting and useful concepts to learn that can be used to design either something highly practical or very creative.

I am a big enjoyer of music and board games, interesting discussions and dissecting how and why things function the way they do. You can never learn anything, they say, but I think that this fact should never stop you from trying.
